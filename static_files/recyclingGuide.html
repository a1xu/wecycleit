Recycling Guide


Cans, Bottles, Glass, Plastic Bottles, Tubs, Jugs, Jars, Containers, Cartons. Make sure the items are all clean and empty! Make sure you don't place them in bags either!

Flowers, Garden Waste, Weeds, Cut up Shrubs, Leaves, Fruit, Cut up tree branches, grass clippings. Make sure none of it is in a plastic bag!

Food containers (aka Styrofoam), plastic bags (even ketchup packets), food, plastic utensils, and shredded paper are allowed here. Keep in mind that there may be alternative recycling options.
